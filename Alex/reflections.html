MIT GSW

I first got to know about MIT GSWin December 2016 when I applied to their business plan competition. Much as I did not make it to the finals, I was invited to MIT GSW 2017

However, in 2018 shortly before attending that year's event in Bangkok, Thailand I put together a team to pitch to MIT and organize this event in Mauritius where we were eventually chosen as one of the 2 finalists in the world to host in 2019 and subsequesntly settling for 2020

I learnt to be very proactive, think and do big as well as work in partnerships with other people. I encourage future students to think outside the box and keep their word because trust is very key when executing such projects that involve teams from anywhere in the world
