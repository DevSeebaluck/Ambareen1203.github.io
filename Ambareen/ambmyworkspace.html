My Workspace

Projects

SIL is an expert company with a team of dedicated professionals. Over and above being seasoned experts certified in key technologies, we deliver IT solutions and services with the highest standards by enhancing technical expertise with sound industry knowledge and market acumen for Mauritius and Africa as well as by focusing on our client’s present and future needs.

SIL’s IT solutions and systems are built on leading technologies from global leaders and they support economic, business and social development on national scales. We deliver projects based on such technologies, so as to optimise the business value that IT can deliver to our clients.

Our experience and expertise span over 28 years and 15 countries in the Indian Ocean/African continent region. With our main office in Mauritius, subsidiary companies in Namibia and Botswana, as well as with our partner in Ghana (for the western African territories), we have successfully developed, implemented and maintained over 250 projects throughout the African continent.

Clients

SIL quality processes are built on 20+ years of business experience and interactions with its Mauritian and African customers. Our Internal Business Process department utilizes our business knowledge to develop quality processes and procedures that are built on best-practices in quality management; and which are designed to continuously improve customer/stakeholder satisfaction.

SIL has a dedicated department to address customer service where the purpose of the Customer Service Department is to provide exceptional class service to customers in the ICT business environment.The customer service helpdesk is a live interface to customers for reporting service requests and queries by phone, fax and email. Such requests are logged onto the database and immediately passed on to the appropriate resource and attended within reasonable delay with constant follow ups
